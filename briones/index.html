Questions
FAQ
Is this an all-inclusive resort?
Yes! Dreams Riviera Cancun is fully all-inclusive — meals, premium drinks, entertainment, and beach access are all included in your room rate.
How do I book my room?
Contact Amy Cate directly at amy.cate@destinationweddings.com or (832) 773-8664. Rooms are booking as individual reservations — she'll handle everything for you.
What airport should I fly into?
Fly into Cancun International Airport (CUN). The resort is approximately 45 minutes south of the airport. Transportation arrangements can be made through Amy.
Do I need a passport?
Yes, a valid US passport is required for travel to Mexico. Make sure your passport is valid for at least 6 months beyond your travel dates.
Is travel insurance recommended?
Absolutely — travel insurance is highly recommended to protect your investment. Ask Amy for details when you book your room.
What is the dress code for the vow renewal?
More details will be shared closer to the date. For the ceremony, elegant resort attire is always appropriate and appreciated!
